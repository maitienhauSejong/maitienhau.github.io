Background

Hugo Verhelst is a PhD student in the Numerical Analysis group (EEMCS) as well as in the Ship and Offshore Structures group (3mE) of Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), under supervision of Mirek Kaminski, Matthias Möller and Henk den Besten. His current work focusses on the development of structural analysis tools for thin membrane structures at sea, using the mathematical concept of Isogeometric Analysis. His contributions are mainly related to the field of computational structural analysis and he is co-developer of the Geometry+Simulation Modules (G+Smo).
